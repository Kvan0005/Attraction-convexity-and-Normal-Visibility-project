Inverse Attraction Regions

Computation

We compute the constraining half-planes of every edge of SPTr (p) of p and the corresponding domain subpolygons. Then, for every region Ri of the shortest path map of p, we compute the free region Freei, where vi is the base vertex of the region; and we add the intersection of Ri and Freei to the inverse attraction region of p.
